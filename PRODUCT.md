# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Developers and content reviewers who inspect AI-generated artifacts and need to leave precise, contextual feedback without interrupting their review flow.

## Product Purpose

ezreview is a local-first browser review companion for AI-generated HTML. It anchors human comments to the relevant content, carries structured feedback back to an agent, and keeps the agent's replies in the same review thread. Success means reviewers can understand, annotate, and approve an artifact without copying screenshots, selectors, or surrounding context by hand.

## Brand Personality

Focused, reliable, and technical. The interface should feel calm and task-oriented while making human and agent state easy to distinguish.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative, marketing-led SaaS presentation; novelty that obscures standard controls; and visual effects that compete with the artifact being reviewed.

## Design Principles

- Keep the artifact, annotation anchor, and conversation visibly connected.
- Preserve the reviewer's flow with familiar, compact controls.
- Make system and agent state explicit instead of surprising.
- Keep user-authored and agent-authored content safe, readable, and faithful to its source formatting.
- Let the reviewed artifact remain the visual focus.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Preserve keyboard-operable controls, visible focus states, readable contrast, safe text rendering, and layouts that remain usable with long or multilingual content.
